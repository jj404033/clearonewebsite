Site Plan

The site plan provides an overhead view of the entire parking lot expansion, highlighting the layout of new parking spaces, entry and exit points, and landscaping features. It serves as the master blueprint guiding all other sections, ensuring that every detail aligns with the overall project vision.

Latitudinal Plan

This project focuses on the latitudinal design, ensuring proper drainage and leveling across the expanded parking lot. By optimizing surface profiles, the design minimizes water pooling and integrates new features like accessible parking and EV chargers.

Longitudinal Plan

The longitudinal plan of the parking lot expansion shows the detailed profile of the terrain modifications and surface elevations along the length of the project area. It includes necessary adjustments to accommodate drainage, ensuring proper water flow and preventing pooling in the new parking spaces. This section highlights the importance of maintaining structural integrity while improving accessibility and functionality of the expanded area.

Sections 1

Section 1 provides cross-sectional details of the initial portion of the parking lot expansion. It illustrates the design of the curbs, drainage, and layering of materials from the subbase to the surface asphalt. This section is crucial for understanding the foundational structure supporting the new parking spaces.

Sections 2

Section 2 continues to detail the cross-sectional views, focusing on the mid-section of the expansion. It includes specific measurements for slope grades and the integration of the new parking lot with existing infrastructure. This section ensures the smooth transition between old and new parts of the parking area.

Sections 3

The final cross-sectional analysis, Section 3, covers the latter part of the expansion project. It focuses on the integration of EV chargers and accessible parking spaces, ensuring compliance with relevant codes and regulations. This section finalizes the structural overview of the parking lot, ensuring a comprehensive design.
