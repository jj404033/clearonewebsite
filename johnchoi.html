About Me

Electrician & Mechanical Engineering Student

John is a first-year mechanical engineering student at the University of Waterloo, with a certification as an industrial/construction electrician. He developed his skills at Ozz Electric, where he gained substantial practical experience in electrical work. His professional experience includes analyzing Invitation for Construction (IFC) and tender drawings, and participating in construction projects like Brentwood Tower 3 & 4. Additionally, John was involved in the building and design of a modern house, acquiring knowledge in standard house framing, overhead service installation, solar panel and wind turbine setups, as well as general wiring tasks. As a founding member of ClearOne Innovators, he aims to be involved in collaborative projects, improve the website, and acquire valuable experience for his future endeavors.
